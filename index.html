About Radhika Academy

Radhika Academy was founded with a vision to provide premium, industry-standard education in the fields of Makeover Artistry, Fashion Designing, Tailoring, and Aari Work. We believe in nurturing talent and turning passion into a successful profession.

Our expert instructors bring years of practical experience into the classroom, ensuring that every student receives personalized attention and hands-on training. Whether you are a beginner looking to start a new career or a professional aiming to upgrade your skills, Radhika Academy is the perfect place for you.
